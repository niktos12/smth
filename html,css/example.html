Best 6 Game Launchers

Steam

There’s no contest if you’re looking for the biggest game library possible. Steam offers access to around 50,000 titles while Epic Games Store came in at under 2,000 – as of 2022. So, if quantity matters to you, then Steam is well ahead.

Epic Games

One of the main attractions of Epic Games Launcher is its regular offering of free games. Epic has also tried to differentiate itself with a more generous revenue-sharing model for game developers, which has the potential to lead to lower prices for consumers.

Ubisoft Connect

A launcher from Ubisoft that provides access to Ubisoft games and services, such as Uplay. Ubisoft games are available in this launcher, including popular franchises by type: Assasin`s Creed, Far Cry and more.

Origin

A launcher from Electronic Arts offering a wide selection of EA games, including the FIFA, Battlefield and The Sims series. It also allows you to access free games and add-ons.

GOG Galaxy

Good Old Games (GOG) Galaxy is a launcher that specializes in classic and DRM-free games. It is popular among players who appreciate retro gaming and freedom from digital restrictions.

Battle.net

A launcher from Blizzard Entertainment that provides access to games such as World of Warcraft, Diablo, Overwatch and other popular projects of this company. It is also used for online games and communication.

Conclusion

Steam continues to be one of the most popular game launchers in the world thanks to its huge catalog of games and social features such as Steam Community and Steam Workshop.

Epic Games Store was able to gain great popularity thanks to free games, exclusive releases and its strategy of attracting players.

Ubisoft Connect remains an integral part of the experience of players who want to play Ubisoft games, and provides access to various Ubisoft services.

Origin (from EA) also remains an important launcher for players interested in Electronic Arts games.

GOG Galaxy finds its niche among players who appreciate classic games and the lack of DRM protection.

Battle.net continues to be a key tool for players playing Blizzard Entertainment games.

The choice of a game launcher depends on your preferences and interests, as well as on which games you prefer to play. Each launcher offers unique features and content, and your choice may depend on which.
